## 目录结构

        public --- 存放一些公共资源
        resource --- 一些打包的资源
        .gitignore --- 
        package-lock.json --- 
        package.json --- 项目配置
        README.md --- 
        src --- 目录
            |- api --- 
            |- assets --- 
            |- components --- 组件
            |- util --- 
            |- storage --- 数据存储，缓存
            |- store --- vuex
            |- App.vue --- 
            |- main.js --- 
            |- router.js --- 路由
            |- pages --- 页面
                |- index.vue --- 首页
                |- home.vue --- header-footer
                |- product.vue --- 产品
                |- detail.vue --- 商品详情 
                |- orderList.vue --- 订单
                |- orderConfirm.vue --- 订单确认 
                |- cart.vue --- 购物车 
                |- orderPay.vue --- 支付页面
                |- login.vue --- 登陆页面 
                |- alipay.vue --- 支付页面跳转到支付宝的中转页面
